PLACES TO VISIT

Lalibela

The eleven Rock-hewn Churches of Lalibela are monolithic churches located in the Western Ethiopian Highlands build in 12th center.

Ertale

Erta Ale is a continuously active basaltic shield volcano in the Afar Region of northeastern Ethiopia. It is situated in the Afar Depression.

Axum

Aksum, powerful kingdom in northern Ethiopia, Northeast Africa and South Arabia from Classical antiquity to the Early Middle Ages.

Blue nile falls

The Blue Nile Falls is a waterfall on the Blue Nile river in Ethiopia. It is known as Tis Abay in Amharic, meaning "great smoke"..
